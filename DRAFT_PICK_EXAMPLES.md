## Rekomendasi Umum

### Tank Supports (Best for ADC Protection)
- **Akai** - Great initiator + CC
- **Tigreal** - Strong area CC
- **Franco** - Single target lock
- **Khufra** - High CC potential

### Mage Supports (Best for Team Damage)
- **Cecilion** - Highest damage output
- **Eudora** - Strong burst + CC
- **Lunox** - Versatile + mobility

### Fighter Synergy (Best for Multi-Role Teams)
- **Arlott** - Balanced damage + CC
- **Hilda** - Great sustain
- **Ruby** - High lifesteal + CC

### Marksman Carries (Best with Tank Support)
- **Miya** - Consistent damage
- **Claude** - High burst
- **Karrie** - Finisher capability
- **Layla** - Long range damage

## Tips untuk Draft Picking

1. **Balanced Composition** - Pastikan punya Tank, Support, Damage dealer (Mage/Marksman/Fighter)
2. **CC Chain** - Pilih heroes yang bisa CC chain untuk team fight advantage
3. **Damage Types** - Mix physical dan magical untuk penetrasi defense enemy
4. **Sustain** - Pastikan punya healer/support untuk team sustainability
5. **Role Coverage** - Avoid duplicate roles dalam 5 drafted heroes jika possible
